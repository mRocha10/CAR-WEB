Škoda

Simply Clever

Historical Overview

Founded in 1895 as Laurin & Klement, Škoda has evolved into one of Europe's most respected automotive manufacturers. Based in Mladá Boleslav, Czech Republic, and part of the Volkswagen Group since 1991, Škoda combines Czech engineering tradition with modern technology to create practical, value-focused vehicles.
