Great Wall Motors

Innovation Drives Excellence

Historical Overview

Founded in 1984, Great Wall Motors has grown from a small truck manufacturer to become China's largest SUV and pickup truck producer. Known for its focus on robust design and value proposition, Great Wall has successfully expanded globally while maintaining its position as a leader in the domestic market.
