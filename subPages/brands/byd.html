BYD

Build Your Dreams

Innovating the future with electric mobility and sustainable technology

Brand History

Founded in 1995, BYD (Build Your Dreams) has grown from a battery manufacturer to become one of the world's leading electric vehicle producers. With innovative technology and sustainable solutions, BYD is revolutionizing the automotive industry through electrification.

BYD's journey began with a vision to create electric vehicles that not only meet but exceed the needs of drivers. The company's initial focus was on battery technology, but it quickly expanded its portfolio to include electric cars, SUVs, and more.

Current Brand Focus

  • Battery Innovation: Developing cutting-edge battery technology
  • Electric Mobility: Expanding global presence with innovative solutions
  • Sustainable Transport: Leading zero-emission transportation
  • Smart Technology: Advancing autonomous driving capabilities
