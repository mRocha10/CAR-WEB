Bentley

Extraordinary Journeys

Brand History

Founded in 1919 by W.O. Bentley, Bentley Motors has become synonymous with British luxury motoring excellence. From its early racing successes at Le Mans in the 1920s to its current status as a maker of the world's finest luxury cars, Bentley has maintained an unwavering commitment to both performance and craftsmanship.

Throughout its history, Bentley has created some of the most iconic luxury vehicles ever made. The company's dedication to handcraftsmanship, combined with cutting-edge technology, has established Bentley as a leader in the ultra-luxury automotive segment. Now part of the Volkswagen Group, Bentley continues to push the boundaries of luxury while preserving its rich heritage.

Current Brand Focus

Bentley is leading the charge in sustainable luxury while maintaining its position as a creator of the world's finest motorcars. The brand's current priorities include:

  • Sustainable Luxury: Transitioning to hybrid and electric powertrains
  • Digital Craftsmanship: Integrating modern technology with traditional skills
  • Bespoke Creation: Offering unprecedented levels of personalization
  • Performance Heritage: Maintaining the perfect balance of luxury and capability
