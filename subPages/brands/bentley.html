Bentley

Extraordinary Journeys

Brand History

Founded in 1919 by W.O. Bentley, Bentley Motors has established itself as the pinnacle of British luxury motoring. From its early racing successes at Le Mans to its modern ultra-luxury vehicles, Bentley combines exceptional craftsmanship with outstanding performance.

Throughout its history, Bentley has maintained an unwavering commitment to excellence, with each vehicle being a testament to the brand's dedication to luxury, performance, and handcrafted quality. The company's heritage of creating powerful grand tourers while maintaining the highest standards of luxury continues to define the brand today.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Bentley is leading the luxury segment into a new era while preserving its core values. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Sustainable Luxury: Pioneering eco-friendly premium mobility
  • Handcrafted Excellence: Maintaining traditional craftsmanship
  • Modern Technology: Integrating cutting-edge innovations
  • Electrification: Developing hybrid and electric powertrains
