McLaren

Pushing the limits of performance, innovation, and aerodynamic excellence

Brand History

Founded by Bruce McLaren in 1963, McLaren has evolved from a racing team to a world-renowned supercar manufacturer. With decades of Formula 1 success and groundbreaking road cars like the F1 and P1, McLaren continues to push the boundaries of automotive performance and technology.

Current Brand Focus

  • Racing Excellence: Maintaining Formula 1 heritage and success
  • Lightweight Innovation: Pioneering carbon fiber technology
  • Performance Engineering: Developing cutting-edge aerodynamics
  • Future Technology: Exploring hybrid powertrains and electrification

  • GT - Grand touring supercar
