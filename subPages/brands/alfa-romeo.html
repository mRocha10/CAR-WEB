Alfa Romeo

Founded in 1910, Alfa Romeo has been synonymous with Italian automotive excellence and racing heritage. From dominating early Grand Prix racing to creating some of the most beautiful road cars ever made, Alfa Romeo combines passion, performance, and distinctive Italian design.

Current Brand Focus

  • Performance DNA: Maintaining racing heritage in road cars
  • Design Excellence: Continuing Italian styling tradition
  • Premium Position: Competing in luxury segments
  • Electrification: Developing new hybrid powertrains
