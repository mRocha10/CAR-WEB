Peugeot

Motion & Emotion

Brand History

Founded in 1810, Peugeot began as a steel foundry before evolving into one of the world's oldest automotive manufacturers. With a legacy spanning over two centuries, Peugeot combines French elegance with cutting-edge technology to create distinctive vehicles that embody their slogan 'Motion & Emotion'.

Current Brand Focus

  • Electrification: Expanding electric and hybrid vehicle range
  • Premium Positioning: Elevating brand image with upscale designs
  • i-Cockpit Innovation: Advanced ergonomic interior design
  • Performance: Balancing efficiency with driving dynamics
