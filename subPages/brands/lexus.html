🏛️ Brand History & Legacy

Founded in 1989 and headquartered in Nagoya, Japan, Lexus has established itself as a significant force in the automotive industry. The brand's commitment to Experience Amazing reflects its core philosophy and dedication to automotive excellence.

Throughout its history, Lexus has consistently delivered vehicles that combine innovation, quality, and performance. The brand's evolution represents decades of engineering expertise and a deep understanding of customer needs across global markets.

Innovation & Excellence

Lexus continues to push boundaries in automotive technology and design, developing vehicles that meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance. The brand's dedication to innovation ensures it remains competitive in an ever-evolving automotive landscape.

Today, Lexus stands as a testament to automotive craftsmanship, offering a diverse lineup of vehicles that cater to various customer preferences while maintaining the brand's core values and commitment to excellence.
