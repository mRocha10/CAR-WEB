Lexus

Experience Amazing

Brand History

Launched in 1989 by Toyota, Lexus was created to develop a luxury car that would challenge the world's best. The result was the LS 400, a vehicle that revolutionized the luxury car segment with its unprecedented quality, comfort, and refinement. This commitment to excellence established Lexus as a formidable player in the premium automotive market.

Over the decades, Lexus has built its reputation on exceptional build quality, advanced technology, and innovative design. The brand pioneered luxury hybrid technology with the RX 400h in 2005, setting new standards for environmental responsibility in the luxury segment.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Lexus continues to push boundaries in luxury automotive innovation while maintaining its core values of exceptional quality and customer service. The brand's current focus areas include:

  • Electrification: Expanding hybrid and all-electric vehicle offerings
  • Takumi Craftsmanship: Preserving traditional Japanese craftsmanship in modern vehicles
  • Design Innovation: Evolving the bold "L-finesse" design philosophy
  • Advanced Safety: Developing cutting-edge safety and driver assistance systems
