Lexus

Brand History

Established in 1989, Lexus emerged as Toyota's luxury vehicle division with a commitment to creating vehicles that combine exceptional quality, sophisticated luxury, and innovative technology. The brand made its debut with the LS 400, which set new standards for luxury sedans with its remarkable refinement and reliability.

Throughout its history, Lexus has consistently pushed the boundaries of luxury automotive excellence. The brand's dedication to craftsmanship, attention to detail, and pursuit of perfection has earned it a reputation for producing some of the most reliable and refined luxury vehicles in the world.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Lexus continues to evolve while maintaining its commitment to luxury and innovation. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Electrification: Expanding hybrid and electric vehicle lineup
  • Craftsmanship: Maintaining Takumi master craftsmanship
  • Design Innovation: Advancing bold styling with L-finesse
  • Advanced Technology: Developing cutting-edge safety and comfort features
