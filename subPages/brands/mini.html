MINI

Born in 1959, MINI revolutionized small car design with its innovative transverse engine layout. Today, under BMW ownership, MINI continues to blend its iconic British heritage with modern technology and premium quality.

Current Brand Focus

  • Design Heritage: Maintaining iconic MINI styling cues
  • Urban Mobility: Creating perfect city-friendly vehicles
  • Driving Fun: Delivering go-kart handling experience
  • Electrification: Developing sustainable urban transport solutions
