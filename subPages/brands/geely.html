Geely

Global Vision, Local Excellence

Brand History

Founded in 1986, Geely has evolved from a local Chinese manufacturer to a global automotive powerhouse. Through strategic acquisitions and technological innovation, Geely has become one of China's most influential automotive groups, owning brands like Volvo Cars and Lotus.

The company's remarkable journey from a regional player to a global force demonstrates its commitment to innovation and excellence in the automotive industry.

Current Brand Focus

  • Global Expansion: Building international presence through strategic partnerships
  • Sustainable Mobility: Developing new energy vehicles and eco-friendly technologies
  • Smart Technology: Advancing connected car solutions and digital integration
  • Design Innovation: Creating distinctive automotive aesthetics and experiences
