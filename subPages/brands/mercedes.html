Mercedes-Benz

The Best or Nothing

Brand History

Mercedes-Benz, founded in 1926, is the world's oldest automotive manufacturer. The brand's origins trace back to Karl Benz's creation of the first petrol-powered car in 1886 and Gottlieb Daimler's pioneering automotive innovations. The iconic three-pointed star symbolizes the company's dominance of land, sea, and air.

Throughout its history, Mercedes-Benz has been synonymous with luxury, innovation, and automotive excellence. From introducing the first diesel passenger car to pioneering numerous safety technologies, the brand has consistently set industry standards.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Mercedes-Benz continues to lead the automotive industry with its commitment to innovation and luxury. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Electric Mobility: Expanding the EQ electric vehicle range
  • Autonomous Driving: Developing advanced driver assistance systems
  • Luxury Innovation: Setting new standards in premium vehicles
  • Sustainable Luxury: Creating environmentally conscious premium vehicles
