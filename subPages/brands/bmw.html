BMW

The Ultimate Driving Machine

Brand History

Founded in 1916, BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) began as an aircraft engine manufacturer before transitioning to motorcycles and automobiles. The company has established itself as a leading manufacturer of premium vehicles, known for their driving dynamics and technological innovation.

Throughout its history, BMW has maintained its reputation for engineering excellence and driving pleasure. From the iconic BMW 328 to the revolutionary BMW i3, the company has consistently pushed the boundaries of automotive technology while staying true to its core values of dynamic performance and premium quality.

Current Brand Focus

Today, BMW continues to lead in premium mobility while embracing future technologies. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Electrification: Expanding the BMW i electric vehicle lineup
  • Driving Dynamics: Maintaining class-leading performance
  • Digital Innovation: Advancing connected car technologies
  • Sustainable Premium: Developing eco-friendly luxury solutions
