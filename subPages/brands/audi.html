Founded in 1909 and headquartered in Ingolstadt, Germany, Audi has established itself as a premium automotive brand synonymous with German engineering excellence. The name "Audi" is Latin for "hear," derived from the founder August Horch's surname which means "listen" in German.

Audi revolutionized automotive technology with the introduction of Quattro all-wheel drive in 1980, forever changing performance and safety standards. This groundbreaking system gave Audi dominance in rally racing and became a cornerstone of the brand's identity.

Innovation & Excellence

The Audi A4, A6, and Q5 represent decades of refinement in luxury, technology, and performance. Audi's commitment to "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Advancement through Technology) is evident in innovations like Virtual Cockpit, Matrix LED headlights, and advanced driver assistance systems.

Today, Audi leads the premium electric vehicle segment with the e-tron lineup, combining zero-emissions driving with the brand's signature luxury and performance.
