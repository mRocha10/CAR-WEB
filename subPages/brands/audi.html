Audi

Vorsprung durch Technik

Brand History

Founded in 1909 by August Horch, Audi has evolved into one of the world's leading premium automotive manufacturers. The four rings in Audi's logo represent the merger of four independent motor vehicle manufacturers: Audi, DKW, Horch, and Wanderer. The company's commitment to innovation and technical excellence is embodied in its slogan "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Progress through Technology).

Throughout its history, Audi has been at the forefront of automotive innovation. The introduction of the quattro all-wheel-drive system in 1980 revolutionized both rally racing and road car performance. This technology remains a cornerstone of Audi's engineering philosophy today.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Audi continues to push the boundaries of automotive technology and design. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Electric Mobility: Expanding the e-tron family of electric vehicles
  • Digital Innovation: Advancing connected car technologies
  • Quattro Evolution: Developing next-generation all-wheel drive systems
  • Sustainable Premium: Creating environmentally conscious luxury vehicles
