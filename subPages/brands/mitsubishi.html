Mitsubishi

Drive your Ambition

Brand History

Founded in 1917 as part of the Mitsubishi Group, Mitsubishi Motors has a rich history of automotive innovation. The company began producing the Model A, Japan's first series-production passenger car, and has since been at the forefront of various technological advancements in the automotive industry.

Mitsubishi has achieved significant success in motorsport, particularly in rally racing with the legendary Lancer Evolution series. The company has also been a pioneer in electric vehicle technology, launching the i-MiEV in 2009 as one of the first mass-produced electric vehicles.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Mitsubishi continues to innovate while focusing on SUVs and environmental sustainability. The brand's key priorities include:

  • Electric Innovation: Developing next-generation electric and hybrid vehicles
  • SUV Excellence: Enhancing crossover and SUV capabilities
  • Advanced Safety: Implementing cutting-edge safety technologies
  • Global Sustainability: Reducing environmental impact across operations
