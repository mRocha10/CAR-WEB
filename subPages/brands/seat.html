SEAT

Founded in 1950, SEAT (Sociedad Española de Automóviles de Turismo) represents Spanish automotive passion and innovation. As part of the Volkswagen Group, SEAT combines German engineering precision with Mediterranean design flair to create dynamic, sporty vehicles for the modern driver.

Current Brand Focus

  • Urban Mobility: Smart solutions for city transportation
  • Sporty Character: Dynamic driving experience across the range
  • Electrification: Developing sustainable mobility solutions
  • Design Innovation: Contemporary Spanish aesthetics
