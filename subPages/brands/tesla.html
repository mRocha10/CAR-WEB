Tesla

Accelerating the World's Transition to Sustainable Energy

Brand History

Founded in 2003 by a group of engineers, Tesla has revolutionized the automotive industry by proving that electric vehicles can be better, quicker, and more fun to drive than gasoline cars. Named after inventor Nikola Tesla, the company's mission has been to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Tesla's journey began with the Roadster in 2008, the world's first premium all-electric sports car. The company then expanded its lineup with the Model S, which redefined the modern luxury sedan. Each subsequent model has pushed the boundaries of electric vehicle technology and autonomous driving capabilities.

Current Brand Focus

Tesla continues to lead the electric vehicle revolution while expanding into energy generation and storage. The company's key priorities include:

  • Advanced Technology: Developing next-generation batteries and autonomous driving systems
  • Manufacturing Innovation: Improving production efficiency and scalability
  • Sustainable Energy: Expanding solar and energy storage solutions
  • Global Expansion: Increasing production capacity and market presence worldwide
