SAIC Motor

Driving Future Mobility

Historical Overview

Founded in 1955, SAIC Motor Corporation Limited (SAIC) has grown to become China's largest automotive manufacturer. Through joint ventures with Volkswagen and General Motors, as well as its own brands like MG and Roewe, SAIC has established itself as a global automotive powerhouse.
