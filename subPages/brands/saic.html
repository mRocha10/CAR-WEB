SAIC Motor

Driving Tomorrow Today

Brand History

Founded in 1955, SAIC Motor Corporation Limited (SAIC) has grown to become China's largest automotive manufacturer. Through joint ventures with Volkswagen and General Motors, as well as its own brands like MG and Roewe, SAIC has established itself as a global automotive powerhouse.

The company's success story is built on strategic partnerships, technological innovation, and a commitment to quality that has earned it a leading position in the global automotive industry.

Current Brand Focus

  • Global Expansion: Strengthening international presence through strategic partnerships
  • Electric Innovation: Leading in new energy vehicle development
  • Connected Mobility: Advancing intelligent driving technologies
  • Brand Portfolio: Managing diverse brands and joint ventures
