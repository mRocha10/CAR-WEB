Cadillac

Founded in 1902, Cadillac has long been synonymous with American luxury, innovation, and prestige. From introducing the first electric starter to pioneering modern driver assistance systems, Cadillac continues to push the boundaries of luxury automotive excellence.

Current Brand Focus

  • Luxury Innovation: Leading the way in premium automotive technology
  • Design Excellence: Creating bold, distinctive American luxury styling
  • Performance Luxury: Developing high-performance V-Series vehicles
  • Electric Future: Transitioning to an all-electric luxury lineup
