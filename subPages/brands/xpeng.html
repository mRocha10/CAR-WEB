Xpeng

Exploring Intelligent Mobility

Brand History

Founded in 2014, Xpeng Motors is at the forefront of China's smart electric vehicle revolution. Combining cutting-edge autonomous driving technology with innovative design, Xpeng is committed to making intelligent EVs accessible to a global market.

The company's rapid growth and technological advancement demonstrate its dedication to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in electric and autonomous vehicles.
