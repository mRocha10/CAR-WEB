Nissan

Innovation That Excites

Brand History

Founded in 1933, Nissan has grown from its Japanese roots to become a global automotive powerhouse. The company's history began as the Jidosha Seizo Co., Ltd., before being renamed to Nissan Motor Co., Ltd. Through the decades, Nissan has built a reputation for innovative engineering and bold design choices.

In 2010, Nissan made history with the introduction of the Leaf, the world's first mass-market electric vehicle. This groundbreaking move established Nissan as a pioneer in electric mobility and demonstrated the company's commitment to sustainable transportation solutions.

Current Brand Focus

Nissan continues to push the boundaries of automotive innovation while expanding its electric vehicle lineup. The company's key priorities include:

  • Electrification: Expanding the range of electric and hybrid vehicles
  • Autonomous Driving: Developing ProPILOT technology and advanced safety systems
  • Connected Services: Enhancing vehicle connectivity and user experience
  • Design Innovation: Creating bold, distinctive vehicle designs
