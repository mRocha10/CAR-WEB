Jeep

Go Anywhere, Do Anything

Brand History

Born in 1941 as a military vehicle, Jeep has evolved into the world's most recognized SUV brand. From its origins as the rugged Willys MB that helped win World War II to today's modern adventure vehicles, Jeep has maintained its reputation for unmatched off-road capability and iconic design.

Throughout its history, Jeep has stayed true to its core values of freedom, adventure, and authenticity. The brand's legendary 4x4 capability, combined with continuous innovation in technology and comfort, has created a unique position in the automotive industry.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Jeep continues to evolve while maintaining its legendary capability. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Off-Road Leadership: Maintaining superior 4x4 capability
  • Electrification: Developing 4xe plug-in hybrid technology
  • Modern Technology: Integrating advanced safety and connectivity
  • Design Heritage: Preserving iconic Jeep styling elements
