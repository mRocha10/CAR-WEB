Lotus

Simplify, Then Add Lightness

Brand History

Founded by Colin Chapman in 1948, Lotus has been guided by the principle of "simplify, then add lightness." Known for its innovative engineering and racing success, Lotus has created some of the world's most engaging sports cars, combining exceptional handling with pure driving pleasure.

Throughout its history, Lotus has achieved remarkable success in Formula 1 and other motorsports, while consistently pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and aerodynamics. This racing heritage continues to influence their road cars today.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Lotus is embracing the future while staying true to its core principles. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Lightweight Engineering: Continuing the tradition of efficient performance
  • Electric Innovation: Developing next-generation electric sports cars
  • Driving Dynamics: Maintaining exceptional handling characteristics
  • Racing Heritage: Applying motorsport technology to road cars
