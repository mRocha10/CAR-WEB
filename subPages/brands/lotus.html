Lotus

Founded by Colin Chapman in 1948, Lotus has been guided by the principle of "simplify, then add lightness." Known for its innovative engineering and racing success, Lotus continues to create some of the world's most engaging sports cars.

Current Brand Focus

  • Lightweight Engineering: Continuing the tradition of efficient performance
  • Electric Innovation: Developing next-generation electric sports cars
  • Driving Dynamics: Maintaining exceptional handling characteristics
  • Racing Heritage: Applying motorsport technology to road cars

Current Model Lineup

Sports Cars

  • Emira - Mid-engine sports car
  • Evija - All-electric hypercar

SUVs & Lifestyle Vehicles

  • Eletre - All-electric luxury SUV
  • Emeya - Electric grand tourer
