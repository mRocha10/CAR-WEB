Honda

The Power of Dreams

Brand History

Founded in 1948 by Soichiro Honda, Honda Motor Co., Ltd. began as a motorcycle manufacturer before expanding into automobiles in 1963. The company's first car, the T360 mini truck, was followed by the S500 sports car, setting the foundation for Honda's reputation for engineering excellence and innovation.

Honda revolutionized the American car market with the introduction of the Civic in 1973, offering superior fuel efficiency during the oil crisis. The launch of the Accord in 1976 further established Honda as a major player in the automotive industry, known for reliability, efficiency, and technological innovation.

Current Brand Focus

Honda continues to innovate across multiple fronts while maintaining its core values of quality and reliability. The company's key priorities include:

  • Electrification: Expanding hybrid and electric vehicle offerings
  • Safety Technology: Advancing Honda Sensing safety features
  • Environmental Responsibility: Reducing environmental impact across operations
  • Performance Innovation: Developing next-generation powertrains
