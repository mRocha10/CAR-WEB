Subaru

Confidence in Motion

Brand History

Founded in 1953, Subaru is the automotive division of Fuji Heavy Industries (now Subaru Corporation). The company's name comes from the Pleiades star cluster, which is represented in the brand's logo. Subaru made history in 1972 by introducing its symmetrical all-wheel drive system, which has become a defining feature of the brand.

Throughout its history, Subaru has built a reputation for creating reliable, capable vehicles that excel in adverse weather conditions. The company's success in rally racing, particularly in the World Rally Championship, has helped establish its performance credentials while demonstrating the capabilities of its all-wheel drive system.

Current Brand Focus

Subaru continues to innovate while maintaining its core values of safety, capability, and reliability. The brand's key priorities include:

  • Safety Technology: Advancing EyeSight driver assistance systems
  • Environmental Responsibility: Expanding hybrid powertrains
  • All-Wheel Drive Excellence: Refining symmetrical AWD technology
  • Adventure Capability: Enhancing off-road performance features
