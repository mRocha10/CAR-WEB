Dodge

Domestic. Not Domesticated.

Brand History

Founded in 1900 by the Dodge brothers, the company has evolved into an iconic American performance brand. From its early days of producing reliable vehicles to becoming a symbol of American muscle cars, Dodge has consistently delivered powerful and bold vehicles that capture the spirit of automotive enthusiasm.

Throughout its history, Dodge has been at the forefront of American performance car culture. The introduction of legendary models like the Charger and Challenger has cemented the brand's reputation for creating vehicles that combine raw power with distinctive styling.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Dodge continues to push the boundaries of performance while embracing modern technology. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Performance Heritage: Maintaining muscle car legacy
  • Modern Technology: Integrating advanced performance tech
  • Bold Design: Creating distinctive American styling
  • Future Performance: Developing electrified performance vehicles
