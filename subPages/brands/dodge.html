Dodge

Founded in 1900 by the Dodge brothers, Dodge has evolved from a reliable automotive supplier to become synonymous with American muscle cars and high-performance vehicles. Today, the brand continues its legacy of bold design and raw power.

Current Brand Focus

  • Performance Heritage: Maintaining the legacy of iconic muscle cars
  • Power Innovation: Developing high-performance powertrains
  • Bold Design: Creating distinctive and aggressive styling
  • Modern Technology: Integrating advanced features with classic muscle

Current Model Lineup

Performance Cars

  • Challenger - Iconic muscle car
  • Charger - Four-door muscle sedan
  • Durango SRT - High-performance SUV

SUVs & Crossovers

  • Durango - Powerful three-row SUV
  • Hornet - Compact performance crossover
