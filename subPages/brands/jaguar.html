Jaguar

Founded in 1922, Jaguar has become one of Britain's most renowned luxury car manufacturers. Known for combining elegant design with sporting performance, Jaguar has created some of the most iconic vehicles in automotive history, from the E-Type to today's all-electric I-PACE.

Current Brand Focus

  • Electrification: Transitioning to an all-electric future
  • Design Excellence: Maintaining distinctive British luxury aesthetics
  • Performance Heritage: Continuing racing and sporting legacy
  • Sustainable Innovation: Developing eco-friendly luxury solutions
