Ford

Built Ford Tough

Brand History

Founded in 1903 by Henry Ford, the Ford Motor Company revolutionized automotive manufacturing with the introduction of the assembly line. This innovation made cars more accessible to the average person and changed the course of industrial history. From the iconic Model T to the legendary Mustang, Ford has been at the forefront of automotive innovation for over a century.

Throughout its history, Ford has maintained its commitment to producing reliable, innovative vehicles for the masses while also creating legendary performance cars. The company's impact on automotive history, from democratizing car ownership to creating the pony car segment with the Mustang, continues to influence the industry today.

Current Brand Focus

Today, Ford continues to innovate while honoring its heritage. The brand's focus areas include:

  • Electrification: Expanding electric vehicle offerings
  • Truck Leadership: Maintaining F-Series dominance
  • Performance Heritage: Continuing Mustang legacy
  • Sustainable Innovation: Developing eco-friendly solutions
