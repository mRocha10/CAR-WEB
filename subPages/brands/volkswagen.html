Brand History

Founded in 1937, Volkswagen has grown from its humble beginnings as the maker of the "People's Car" to become one of the world's largest automotive manufacturers. Known for combining German engineering excellence with practical innovation, Volkswagen continues to shape the future of mobility.

Current Brand Focus

  • Electric Mobility: Leading the charge with ID series electric vehicles
  • Sustainability: Commitment to carbon-neutral production
  • Digital Integration: Advanced connectivity and user experience
  • Affordable Innovation: Making advanced technology accessible
